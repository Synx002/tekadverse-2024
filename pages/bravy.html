Barvy team aimed at making financial education more engaging and approachable. We’re working together to turn complex financial concepts into easy-to-digest, visually appealing content that’s both informative and fun. Through creative character design, clever storytelling, and impactful visuals, our team is helping bring financial literacy to life in a way that resonates with Instagram audiences.

Barvy ⎯ Gatot, Trivia, & Joni's slice of life, telling relatable stories of finance in daily basis.

Gatot, Trivia, and Joni’s slice of life comic strip brings everyday moments to life with a twist—each story is deeply connected to financial matters. Through their daily activities, readers get a fun and relatable glimpse into how personal finance plays a role in all aspects of life, from budgeting for lunch to making big decisions about savings and spending.

By following these three characters, the comic offers a lighthearted yet insightful way to explore financial topics. Whether it’s Gatot trying to stick to a budget, Trivia’s latest investment idea, or Joni navigating the challenges of managing money, each story is designed to educate and entertain, making finance feel more approachable and part of everyday life.

Our studio takes a collaborative and thoughtful approach to creating this comic strip, starting with the script. We work closely with the client to break down the financial concepts into digestible, relatable stories.

The client came to us with a core concept focused on a specific financial case they wanted to explore in the comic strip. They provided scenarios around complex concepts like business tax strategies, EBITDAM, IPOs, and investment portfolios. Our task was to take these advanced financial ideas and translate them into engaging, relatable stories that the characters encounter in their everyday lives, making these technical topics accessible and entertaining for the audience.

  • Script Writing ⎯ the script focuses on weaving everyday situations with financial lessons, ensuring the dialogue is casual, fun, and easy to follow. Each scenario is carefully crafted to resonate with readers, so they can relate to the characters while learning important financial insights without feeling overwhelmed.
  • Character Design ⎯ bringing Gatot, Trivia, and Joni to life with unique, appealing personalities that reflect their different approaches to money. We aim to make the characters not only visually distinctive but also relatable in their actions and expressions.
  • Comic Creation ⎯ developing dynamic layouts, playful panel designs, and engaging visuals that complement the storytelling. By combining sharp, clean artwork with a lighthearted tone, we ensure the comic strip is both informative and entertaining, making financial education feel fun and accessible for everyone.
